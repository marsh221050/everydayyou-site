The 1% Study Smarter Way

The 1% Study Smarter Way is a calm, practical guide for students who want to study well—without burning out. Forget endless cramming or colour-coded routines. This book shows you how to focus, revise and stay motivated in just 15 minutes a day. With short chapters, science-backed techniques and a flexible 30-day challenge, you’ll learn to build habits that actually last. Whether you’re overwhelmed, struggling a bit or just need a smarter way in, this is your gentle nudge forward. No pressure. No guilt. Just one small step at a time.
